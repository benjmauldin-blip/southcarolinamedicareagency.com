Frequently Asked Questions

Your Medicare Advantage Questions, Answered

What is Medicare Advantage in South Carolina?

Medicare Advantage (Part C) is an all-in-one alternative to Original Medicare offered by private insurers. Plans bundle Part A, Part B, and usually Part D, and often add dental, vision, hearing, and fitness benefits. Many SC plans have $0 monthly premiums.

What Medicare Advantage plans are available in SC?

Multiple major carriers offer plans in South Carolina including Humana, Aetna, UnitedHealthcare, and Blue Cross Blue Shield of SC. We represent 6 organizations offering 52 products in our area. Call 843-509-2462 for a free comparison at your specific zip code.

Is Medicare Advantage better than Original Medicare in SC?

It depends on your situation. Medicare Advantage often has lower premiums and extra benefits but requires using in-network providers. Original Medicare lets you see any Medicare-accepting doctor nationwide. A local independent agent can compare both options for your specific needs.

How do I enroll in Medicare Advantage in South Carolina?

Enroll during your Initial Enrollment Period (7 months around your 65th birthday), Annual Enrollment Period (Oct 15–Dec 7), or a Special Enrollment Period if you qualify. Working with Mauldin Insurance Group costs you nothing.

Can I switch Medicare Advantage plans in South Carolina?

Yes. During Annual Enrollment (Oct 15–Dec 7) you can switch plans, return to Original Medicare, or change Part D coverage. The Medicare Advantage Open Enrollment Period (Jan 1–Mar 31) allows one additional switch per year. Call 843-509-2462 for help reviewing your options.
