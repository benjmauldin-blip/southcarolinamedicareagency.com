Medicare Questions, Answered

Common Medicare Questions

Medicare Advantage (Part C) replaces Original Medicare with an all-in-one plan from a private insurer — often including drug coverage and extras like vision and dental. Medicare Supplement (Medigap) works alongside Original Medicare to fill coverage gaps like deductibles and coinsurance. Advantage plans often have lower premiums but use provider networks. Supplement plans have higher premiums but let you see any Medicare-accepting doctor nationwide.
Your Initial Enrollment Period runs from 3 months before to 3 months after your 65th birthday. If you're still working and covered by employer insurance from a 20+ employee company, you may be able to delay without penalty. Missing your enrollment window without a qualifying reason can result in permanent late enrollment penalties. Call us before your 65th birthday to make sure you don't miss your window.
