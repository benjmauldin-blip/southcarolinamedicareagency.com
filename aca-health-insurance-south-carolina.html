What Is the ACA Marketplace?

Health Insurance You Can Actually Afford

The Affordable Care Act (ACA) created the Health Insurance Marketplace, where individuals and families can shop for comprehensive health coverage. Depending on your income, you may qualify for Premium Tax Credits that significantly reduce your monthly premium — sometimes to $0.

As independent agents, we help you compare every plan available in South Carolina and identify every subsidy you're eligible for. Most people are surprised at how much financial assistance is available.

Open Enrollment runs November 1 through January 15 in South Carolina. Special Enrollment Periods are available if you experience a qualifying life event — like losing job-based coverage, getting married, or having a baby.

  • All plans cover essential health benefits
  • Pre-existing conditions cannot affect your eligibility or rate
  • Premium Tax Credits available based on household income
  • Cost-sharing reductions for qualifying lower-income households
  • Open Enrollment: Nov 1–Jan 15 each year
  • Special Enrollment Periods for qualifying life events
Plan Tiers Explained

Bronze, Silver, Gold — What's the Difference?

🥉

Bronze Plans

Lowest monthly premiums, highest out-of-pocket costs. Best for people who rarely use healthcare and want catastrophic coverage protection.

🥈

Silver Plans

Middle ground on premiums and costs. Silver is the only tier eligible for cost-sharing reductions if your income qualifies. Often the best value.

🥇

Gold Plans

Higher premiums but lower out-of-pocket costs when you use care. Best for people who have regular medical needs or take multiple prescriptions.
