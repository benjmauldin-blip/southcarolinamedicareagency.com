Your Initial Enrollment Period is a 7-month window: 3 months before the month you turn 65, your birthday month, and 3 months after. Miss it without creditable coverage and permanent late-enrollment penalties can apply. We help Blythewood and Northeast Columbia residents stay ahead of every deadline.
Employer plans with 20+ employees usually allow you to delay Part B without penalty. Smaller employer plans, COBRA, and retiree coverage follow different rules. We review your specific employer setup before recommending anything — errors here are permanent and expensive.
AEP runs from October 15 through December 7 each year. Changes you make during AEP — switching Advantage plans, moving between Advantage and Original Medicare, or changing Part D drug plans — take effect January 1. Free annual reviews are part of our service for Blythewood clients.
