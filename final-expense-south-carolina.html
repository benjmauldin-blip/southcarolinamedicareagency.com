What Final Expense Insurance Covers

Final expense policies are small whole life insurance plans designed to cover the costs your family will face at end of life.

⚰️ Funeral & Burial Costs

The average funeral in South Carolina costs $7,000–$12,000. Final expense insurance ensures your family can cover these costs without dipping into savings or going into debt.

🏥 Medical Bills

End-of-life medical expenses can accumulate quickly. The death benefit can be used by your beneficiary for any outstanding medical bills or hospice costs.

💳 Outstanding Debts

Credit cards, personal loans, or other debts don't disappear at death — they become your family's problem. Final expense coverage provides funds to settle these obligations.

🏠 Any Final Expense

Unlike burial-specific policies, final expense life insurance pays a cash death benefit your beneficiary can use for any purpose — including rent, groceries, or any other immediate need.

Why Final Expense Insurance Makes Sense

Simple coverage designed specifically for seniors who want to protect their families from financial stress.

✅

No Medical Exam Required

Most final expense plans require only a few health questions — no blood draws, no physicals, no waiting on lab results. Many seniors with health conditions can still qualify.

🔒

Locked-In Premiums

Your monthly premium never increases. A whole life policy that starts at $40/month stays at $40/month for life — no matter how old you get or if your health changes.

♾️

Coverage That Never Expires

Unlike term life insurance, final expense policies don't expire after a set period. Coverage stays in force as long as you pay premiums — guaranteed.

💰

Affordable Monthly Premiums

Coverage amounts of $5,000–$25,000 keep monthly premiums affordable — typically $20–$100/month depending on your age, health, and coverage amount.

The terms are often used interchangeably, but there's a technical difference. Burial insurance (also called preneed insurance) is purchased through a funeral home and is tied to specific funeral services. Final expense insurance is a life insurance policy that pays a cash death benefit to your beneficiary — who can use it for funeral costs or anything else. Final expense policies offer more flexibility.
